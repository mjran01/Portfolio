About Me

I'm a full-stack developer based in Valenzuela City, Philippines, building scalable web applications, APIs, and digital platforms that solve real-world problems. I work across the entire stack — designing databases, developing backend systems, and crafting interfaces with a focus on clarity, performance, and systems built to handle high-volume usage reliably.

Part of my work involves developing solutions for local government operations, including driver registration portals, attendance monitoring systems, and internal project management platforms used to track city projects, budgets, contractors, timelines, and completion status. These projects strengthened my experience in building production-grade systems capable of managing large datasets, concurrent users, and demanding operational workflows without compromising speed or stability.

Beyond government projects, I also take on freelance work ranging from WordPress websites to custom booking systems and business platforms. I enjoy building software that is practical, maintainable, and designed for long-term growth — with clean, well-structured code that both clients and future developers can work with easily.
