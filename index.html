About Me

I'm a full-stack developer building digital infrastructure for local government in Valenzuela City, Philippines. I work across the entire stack — designing databases, writing APIs, crafting interfaces — with a bias toward clarity and systems that actually work.

My current focus is on civic tech: modernizing how LGU offices manage data, register citizens, and deliver services. I've shipped driver registration portals, market price monitoring systems, and custom admin dashboards — all production-grade.

Beyond government work, I take on freelance projects ranging from WordPress builds to custom booking systems. I write clean, well-commented code because future-me is always reading it.
